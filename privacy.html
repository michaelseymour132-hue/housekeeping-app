Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

Who we are

This application ("Housekeeping Hub", "the service") is operated by [COMPANY NAME] ("we", "us", "our"). It is a private tool for authorized housekeeping staff. For any privacy question or request, contact us at support@housekeepinghub.app.

What we store

We store the information needed to run housekeeping operations: staff names and email addresses; role and hotel assignments; records of cleaning, inspection and deep-clean activity (including timestamps and who performed them); room notes and maintenance reports; and photographs uploaded by staff of rooms, issues, and deep-clean before/after states.

What we do not collect

We do not run third-party advertising or analytics trackers, we do not build advertising profiles, and we do not sell personal data. We do not knowingly collect guest personal data beyond what staff choose to record in operational notes.

Lawful basis

We process this data on the basis of legitimate interests (operating and improving the housekeeping service) and, for staff records, the performance of the employment relationship. We do not use the data for advertising and we do not sell it.

Retention

Operational records are retained while your organisation uses the service and for [RETENTION PERIOD] afterwards, unless a longer period is required by law. Deactivated staff profiles are retained for audit purposes but marked inactive.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or request deletion of your personal data. Administrators can export data from Settings; to request access or deletion, contact support@housekeepinghub.app and we will respond within the period required by applicable law.

Security

Access requires an authenticated account and is protected by Firebase Authentication. Credentials are encrypted in transit. Data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication, and Storage), and access is restricted by role and by organisation membership.

Data processors

We use Google Firebase to host data and handle authentication and file storage, and Vercel to serve the application. These providers process data on our behalf under their respective data-processing terms.

Contact

Questions or requests: support@housekeepinghub.app, [COMPANY ADDRESS].
